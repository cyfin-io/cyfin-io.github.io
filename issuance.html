Issuance of Credit to Customers

Put crypto-conditions to work.

Whenever an economic relationship begins, the underlying asset and the rules of exchange have to be defined. CyFin proposes crypto-conditions and -fulfillments for this negotiation. Two approaches are possible:

- Upon payment, the issuer can create a crypto-fulfillment of a specific value to the paying customer. When the crypto-condition is claimed by the customer the amount stated in the condition is added to his account balance.

- The issuer can create an open crypto-fulfillment, claimable by anyone. Open fullfillment can be put as digital downloads into an online store, or behind a payment wall. The Buyer can complete the fullfillment with his account address and claim the balance.
