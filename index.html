CyFin: Monetize your IoT service through crypto-payment-channels!

Learn More

Integrate

Robots, oracles, and other IoT agents are unable to accept credit cards or Paypal. CyFin provides them equivalent financial abilities using crypto-conditions and crypto-payment-channels.

Issue Credit

Issue credit to your customers upon payment that can be used only with your IoT agent. Anchor the value to the price of national currencies like the US dollar, the Euro, and the Yen.

Meter Usage

Easily submit usage records into crypto-payment-channels. Accounts will be debited regularly with proof of digital signatures and by use of blockchain technology.
